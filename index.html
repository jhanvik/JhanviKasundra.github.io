About me

Passionate about unraveling the stories hidden within data, I am Jhanvi Kasundra, a versatile data enthusiast with a keen eye for detail and a knack for transforming raw information into actionable insights. With a Master's degree in Engineering Science, specializing in Data Science, coupled with hands-on experience as a Research Analyst, Data Engineer, and Data Analyst, I have honed my skills across the data spectrum. From delving into intricate SQL queries to crafting captivating visualizations in Tableau, Power BI and Python, I thrive on leveraging my technical expertise to drive impactful solutions. Whether it's identifying critical patterns in juvenile data to reduce recidivism rates or pioneering algorithms within virtual reality frameworks to enhance learning outcomes, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of data-driven innovation. My journey is fueled by a relentless curiosity and a commitment to making a tangible difference through data-driven decision-making. Outside of the data realm, I am an avid problem-solver, as evidenced by securing first place in the National-Level Smart India Hackathon and contributing to research publications. With a portfolio brimming with diverse projects spanning data science, engineering, and analysis, I am excited to embark on new challenges and continue my quest for excellence in the ever-evolving world of data.
